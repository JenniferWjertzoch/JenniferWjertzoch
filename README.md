## About Me

I'm a Senior Frontend Engineer specialized in the Vue ecosystem (Vue 3, Composition API, Vuex, Nuxt), with a strong foundation in modern JavaScript (ES6+) and TypeScript. I build maintainable, high-performance, and accessible web applications for financial services, with a focus on quality, architecture, and long-term impact.

My journey began in e-commerce, where I discovered the power of translating creative ideas into tangible digital products. Over time, I developed a deep passion for accessibility, not as a checklist, but as a discipline that intersects with software architecture, risk management, and user trust. At DKB Code Factory, I drive frontend innovation by building scalable Vue applications, contributing to a company-wide design system, and actively participating in the Accessibility Guild to establish shared standards across teams.

Beyond delivery, I'm an active voice in the tech community: I write about accessibility architecture in my LinkedIn newsletter (300+ subscribers), have spoken at WP Accessibility Day and internal DKB conferences, and will be speaking at the WeAreDevelopers World Congress in July 2026. For four years (2021–2025), I mentored and coached junior developers at CareerFoundry, supporting career changers into tech through 1:1 sessions, code reviews, and technical guidance.
